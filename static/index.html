Manual Connect ZBT-1 Firmware Upgrade

Notice: All devices can now be updated straight from Home Assistant as of 2025.4.0. Manual firmware installation through the web flasher is not recommended for Home Assistant SkyConnect and Home Assistant Connect ZBT-1 devices purchased before October 20, 2024.

To manually update your Home Assistant Connect ZBT-1 firmware, plug the stick to your current computer and click on the Connect button below.
